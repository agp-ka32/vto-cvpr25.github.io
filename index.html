Visual Modeling Challenges
for 2D-3D Virtual Try-On

CVPR 2025 Workshop

Seattle, WA | June 30, 2025

Overview

This workshop aims to bridge the gap between 2D image-based and 3D-aware virtual try-on systems, addressing fundamental challenges in garment representation, human-body alignment, and photorealistic synthesis. Join us to explore cutting-edge solutions with leading experts in the field.
